ABOUT US

Welcome

I'm Anna Sweeting, Founder of Exuma Elopements, thank you for reaching out to us.

Although my University degree is in public relations, I found my passion in floral design which lead to my introduction to weddings.

For the past eight years I’ve worked as a floral designer and wedding coordinator. And I am proud to offer the Exuma Elopements wedding experience to you.

I am grateful to each couple, who trust Exuma Elopements to organize one of the biggest moment of their lives.

Exuma Elopements is a small, personalized business. We work closely with you to create a high standard, stylish and unique design for every wedding.

I am excited to meet you and be a part of your special day.

Exuma Elopements team

We are a close-knit team, that works seamlessly as one. We are perfectionists, world experienced, positive, creative and love what we do.

We provide elopement packages, which are expertly planned, delivering a most memorable and adventurous wedding experience in Exuma.

You’ll meet the Exuma Elopements team prior to your wedding and we will be with you through your entire wedding event.

Our service also includes all necessary inspections and venue walk-throughs in order to guarantee the event organization.
